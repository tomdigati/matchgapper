Features

Every part of the captain's job, in one clean workflow.

From roster import to locked pairings, MatchGapper handles the invisible labor so you can focus on the match.

Roster import

Upload CSV or Excel from your GHIN portal or club records. Re-import anytime — existing members are matched and updated, never duplicated.

Recruitment status

One click to mark a player Contacted, Available, Maybe, or Declined. Filter the roster to see exactly where recruitment stands.

Week-by-week availability

Track Yes, No, and Maybe across every match week, plus home and away location preferences per player.

Handicap-based team pools

Players are automatically sorted into up to 6 teams by handicap band. Mismatches surface as clear warnings so you stay in control.

Drag-and-drop pairings

Build foursomes visually. Archetype labels (balanced, anchor/floater, top-heavy) help you understand each pairing at a glance.

Lock & distribute

Lock pairings, then copy a polished summary, open your mail app pre-filled, or export a formatted .xlsx. Your call.

How it works

From roster to locked lineup in six steps.

  1. 1

    Upload your roster

    Drop in your GHIN export or club spreadsheet. Names, handicaps, and contact info are ready to go.

  2. 2

    Configure your season

    Set your match weeks, pick your opponents and tee times, and choose how many of your teams you're fielding each week.

  3. 3

    Recruit & track responses

    Work the roster the way you already do. Update each player's status with a click as replies come in.

  4. 4

    Collect availability

    Mark Yes, No, or Maybe per week and capture home or away preferences. Everything surfaces in the team builder.

  5. 5

    Build pairings

    The week of the match, open the drag-and-drop builder. Team pools, archetypes, and conflict warnings guide you to the right lineup.

  6. 6

    Lock & send

    Freeze the lineup, then copy, email, or export. Players and opposing captains get a clean, consistent format every time.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do players need to create accounts?

No. Only captains and vice captains sign in. Players receive pairings via email or a copy-pasted summary — no apps and no logins required.

Which clubs and leagues does MatchGapper support?

MatchGapper is purpose-built for GAP (Golf Association of Philadelphia) inter-club match play. Every workflow is shaped around handicap-based team eligibility and supports up to 6 teams per club across a 3-week season. Support for other regional associations is on the roadmap.

Can I import my existing roster?

Yes. We recommend using the eligible players roster exported from your club's GAP portal, which reflects the April 1 drop of active handicaps. Upload it as CSV or Excel. Any newly eligible players can be added manually through the Add Player form. Re-import whenever handicaps update — existing players are matched by GHIN number and refreshed, not duplicated.

How does the team builder handle handicaps?

Available players are split into team pools based on handicap bands. Team 1 gets the lowest indexes, Team 2 the next band, and so on — up to 6 teams. Each pool targets 12 players (three groups of four). Mismatches or availability conflicts show up as warnings, so you're always in control.

Can multiple captains work on the same club?

Yes. Vice captains share the same club and can update recruitment and availability in real time. Changes sync instantly — no overwriting spreadsheets.

What does the final lineup look like when I send it?

A clean formatted summary with names, handicaps, GHIN numbers, and member numbers — everything the opposing captain needs. Choose copy-to-clipboard, open in Mail, or download a formatted .xlsx.
